Lobster Thermidor

A classic French dish featuring succulent lobster meat in a creamy, flavorful sauce.

Our Lobster Thermidor is made with fresh lobster, mushrooms, cognac, and a hint of mustard, all baked to perfection with a golden cheese crust. Served with a side of buttery mashed potatoes.

Beef Wellington

A luxurious dish of tender beef filet wrapped in puff pastry.

Our Beef Wellington features a succulent beef tenderloin coated with pâté and duxelles, encased in a flaky puff pastry. Served with a rich red wine sauce and a side of roasted vegetables.

Vegetarian Platter

A delightful assortment of fresh, seasonal vegetables.

Our Vegetarian Platter includes grilled vegetables, a quinoa salad, stuffed bell peppers, and a selection of dips such as hummus and tzatziki. Perfect for a healthy and satisfying meal.

Seafood Paella

A traditional Spanish dish packed with fresh seafood and saffron rice.

Our Seafood Paella is made with a blend of shrimp, mussels, clams, and squid, cooked with saffron-infused rice and a medley of vegetables. A true taste of Spain in every bite.

Chocolate Lava Cake

An indulgent dessert with a rich, molten chocolate center.

Our Chocolate Lava Cake is served warm with a gooey, molten chocolate center, accompanied by a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of raspberry sauce. A perfect ending to your meal.
